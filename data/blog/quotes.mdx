# Quotes

A collection of my favourite quotes, in no particular order, that I hope will guide me.

1.

> I disapprove of what you say, but I will defend to the death your right to say it.

<p className="text-sm text-gray-500">
  <i>- Friends of Voltaire</i>, [Evelyn Beatrice
  Hall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evelyn_Beatrice_Hall)
</p>

2.

> I slept and dreamt that life was joy.<br/>
> I awoke and saw that life was service.<br/>
> I acted and behold, service was joy.

<p className="text-sm text-gray-500">
  [- Rabindranath Tagore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rabindranath_Tagore)
</p>

3.

> If there is anything which it is the duty of the whole people to never entrust to any hands but their own, that thing is the preservation and perpetuity of their own liberties and institutions.

<p className="text-sm text-gray-500">
  [- Abraham Lincoln](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rabindranath_Tagore)
</p>
